Climate Summit Madrid 2019: the menu of El Celler de Can Roca to remove consciences

The Roca brothers have designed the dishes that the prime ministers will taste, Heads of State and Government and the Secretary General of the United Nations at the welcome meeting of the Climate Summit.

The sustainable menu of El Celler de Can Roca Those in Girona are going to propose a menu that appeals to conscience and that refers to some of the great environmental problems that will be faced during the summit. “On the menu there are dishes that refer to the I replace meat consumption with vegetable protein products, we talk about the oceans, we talk about drought. We pose a game with these themes so that through ingenuity and creativity we can give messages and raise awareness, ”explains Joan, the eldest of the Roca.

‘Earth runs out. A menu for awareness ’

CLEAR WATER & DIRTY WATER

  • The message: Global water problem
  • The proposal: Freeze-dried broth of death trumpets, truffles, boletus edulis and roasted chickpeas. Transparent vegetable water at 100º C poured on the plate to become a dirty, but tasty broth.

INVASIVE VARIETIES IN AN UNCONTROLLED BOTANICAL MIGRATION

  • The message: Preservation of biodiversity
  • The proposal: Opuntia ficus of the Cap de Creus Natural Park and Blue Crab of the Ebro Delta Natural Park

HOT SEA. EAT UNBALANCE

  • The message: Temperature increase in the oceans.
  • The proposal: Sea and vegetable mountain. Plantain mousse, salicornia, seaweed, Gigartina pistillata, Fennel, sea fennel, enoki, pumpkin seed, basil, maritime Lobularia and flowers.
