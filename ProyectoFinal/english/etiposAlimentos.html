Foods

Foods are found in nature and are what provide us with nutrients. A food can not provide all the essential nutrients, so it is necessary to make a balanced diet.
Food can be classified in different ways according to the classification criteria used.
Depending on the function they have in our body, they are divided into:

  • Simple: are formed by a single type of nutrient. Example: water, salt.
  • Compounds: consist of several types of nutrients. They are the vast majority.
    For example: fruits mainly provide vitamins and mineral salts that are regulatory,
    but they are also rich in carbohydrates that provide energy.

Food classification

Food and food types are grouped taking into account the predominant nutrients and based on this it has been established
the so-called "Food Wheel" that contains seven food groups.
These are:

  • Group 1: Milk and Derivatives. They are plastic foods. Proteins predominate in them.
  • Group 2: Meats, Fish and Eggs. Plastic foods. Proteins predominate in them.
