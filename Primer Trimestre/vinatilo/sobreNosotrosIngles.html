About Us



Since 1995, this “intensive care music unit” prescribes the most active principles of Pop, Rock, Soul, Funk, Jazz, Electronics,                                 Dub, Reggae, Hip Hop, Experimental, Flamenco and other sounds difficult to classify. News, reissues, offers, basic classics or                                 oddities of collecting.

                                The main objective of Pacemaker Discs is to stimulate the musical appetite, to announce something unpublished and fascinating. Facilitate the difficult,                                 Get that dream piece. Advise you as the telepathic compass of a magical “advanced search engine”. Experts in recommending lovers of                                 music and in ordering records.

                                A very complex task that these veteran doctors dispense by spreading passion, with an exquisite and professional treatment.

                                Pacemaker discs have always worked with the vinyl format, even when it agonized and was considered lost.                                 They participate with the Record Store Day since its first national edition in 2011. They have also organized cultural events, record presentations,                                  books.

                                And is that Lola and Pepe have managed to go ahead as true pioneers, following in the footsteps of their intuitive "rhythm radar".                                 Tireless fighters, both in the moments of maximum splendor of the record business and in the most critical and difficult of it.

                                

                                Thus, in 2009 Pacemaker Pacelets opens an online reference store, www.discosmarcapasos.com, and in 2015 a dazzling                                 and spacious premises, as cozy as a dream house, which has allowed them to continue to increase the catalog bottom not only on records,                                 also in books, t-shirts, turntables, and various accessories. Thousands of carefully selected references flood their coverage                                 wooden shelves, a very present element that brings warmth to a store that has always pursued quality.
